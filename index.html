Studio Ethos

At AK Home Design Studio, we do not merely build structures; we curate lifestyles. Based in Bhilai, our architecture harmonizes the sacred geometries of traditional Indian Vastu with unapologetic, modern minimalism.

From central courtyards that breathe natural light into the home, to rich teakwood and custom modular execution—every line drawn is a commitment to spatial poetry and supreme quality.
